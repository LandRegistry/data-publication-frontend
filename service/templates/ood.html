Introduction

The overseas dataset will provide details of any freehold or leasehold title where the registered legal owner is a company that is incorporated outside of the UK (i.e. an overseas company). It currently contains approximately 100,000 titles.

It will include:

  • Title number
  • Land or property address
  • Administrative area
  • Name and correspondence address of the legal owner(s)
  • Country or territory of incorporation
  • Date the owner was registered at Land Registry
  • Title number's tenure (freehold/leasehold)

Download packages

Two download packages allow you to keep your data updated and refreshed. Options include:

  • Full download: a one-off download of the complete England and Wales (E&W) data
  • Change only updates: a file detailing all of the changes since the previous update (either the Full file or previous change only update)
