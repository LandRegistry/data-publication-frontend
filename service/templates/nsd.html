Introduction

Our National Spatial Dataset contains more than 3.2 million polygons representing Registered title records of freehold and leasehold property in England and Wales. You can access all polygons and link this data via Title Number to Tenure, UPRN etc. This chargeable, licensed data includes:

  • Polygon Geometry
  • Title Number

Download packages

Three download packages allow you to choose how frequently you would like your data updated and refreshed. Options include:

  • Single download: a one-off download of the complete England and Wales (E&W) data
  • Half-yearly and quarterly refresh: a complete download of the E&W data at half yearly or quarterly intervals
  • Premium: a complete download of the E&W data, quarterly refreshes and monthly ‘change only’ updates

Data benefits

  • one licence
  • a choice of three download packages allowing you to select the option that best meets your needs
  • ability to use the data internally or reuse with your end clients
  • easy access to secure data rooms
  • low cost data supply charges and a choice of reuse models help to deliver value for money
  • our ‘data test drive’ initiative allows you to review and test a sample sub-set of the data internally, before purchasing the full licenced dataset
  • data is supplied in shapefile format to allow easy integration with your systems and other datasets

Set-up process

  • You will need to sign the licence, select the appropriate download package and confirm whether the data will be used internally only or for external reuse purposes
  • Following receipt of the data supply charge (upfront fee), you will be given access to your data package via a secure data room

Payment

  • annual data supply charge
  • quarterly royalties based on transactional title use or per end user registration
